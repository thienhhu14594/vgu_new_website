Presidential Board

The University Council (UC) is the university institution with the widest ranging of authority. It is appointed by the Minister of Education and Training and consists of 20 representatives from the government, academia, business and society of Vietnam and Germany. Ten of its members are nominated by the Ministry of Education and Training, while another ten are named by the Hessen State Mi nistry of Higher Education, Research and the Arts.
